Box League

BoxLeague.net is an online service for tennis and other racquet-sport leagues – pretty much any sport where two players or two doubles teams compete with each other.

Club administrators set up and manage leagues. Players can log in to view standings, input match results, contact other players, and keep private notes about opponents.
