Junior Tennis Equipment Guide

Choosing the right tennis equipment helps young players build proper technique and enjoy the game. Below are two helpful guides covering racquet sizes and court formats for kids.

1. Junior Racquet Sizes

Use this chart to select the correct racquet size based on your child's height and age. Using the proper size supports better form and comfort.

Racquet Size Age Range
17 inch 2–3 years
19 inch 3–4 years
21 inch 4–6 years
23 inch 6–8 years
25 inch 9–12 years

2. Court Sizes & Ball Types

Courts are scaled to suit young players, making it easier to rally and develop skills. Different court sizes—red, orange, and green—are used as children grow and improve.

Source: USTA Florida
