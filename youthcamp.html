Youth Summer Camp 2025

Our Youth Camp is designed for kids ages 5 to 12 who want to have fun and improve their tennis skills this summer. Led by Yana, a Tennis Canada-certified instructor, campers will enjoy games, skill-building exercises, and plenty of tennis activities in a supportive environment.

Camp Sessions

  1. July 7–11 (Mon–Fri) — SOLD OUT
  2. July 14–18 (Mon–Fri)
  3. July 21–25 (Mon–Fri)
  4. July 28–August 1 (Mon–Fri)
  5. August 5–8 (Tue–Fri)
  6. August 11–15 (Mon–Fri)

Camp Details

  • Coach: Yana (Tennis Canada-certified instructor)
  • Age groups: 5–8 and 9–12 years old
  • Schedule: Camps run Monday to Friday, 9 a.m. – Noon
  • Camps run subject to interest (minimum 6 participants)
  • Please bring snacks and a water bottle

Fees & Registration

$85 per week per child (4-day week Aug 5–8: $68)

Camp fees can be paid by e-transfer directed to the club email address: lethtennisclub@gmail.com

Please include in your e-transfer notice:

  1. Dates of session requested
  2. Name of each player being registered
  3. Email address for each player registered
  4. Parent/Guardian cell number for each player registered
