Club Rules

To ensure the safety, comfort, and enjoyment of all members and guests, the following rules apply at the Lethbridge Tennis Club.

Membership & Guests

  • Only registered members with valid memberships may access the courts.
  • Guests must be accompanied by a member and are required to pay the guest fee.
  • Members are responsible for the behavior of their guests while on club property.

Court Use

  • Courts are available on a first-come, first-served basis.
  • When courts are full, players are encouraged to:
    • Join others for doubles, if appropriate.
    • Wait until a court becomes available.
  • Please limit playtime during busy periods to allow fair court access for all.

Facilities & Conduct

  • Food and drinks (except water) are not permitted on court.
  • All members are expected to clean up after themselves and dispose of garbage properly.
  • Smoking and alcohol are strictly prohibited on club premises.
  • Pets are not allowed on or near courts.

General Expectations

  • Respect other members, staff, and club property at all times.
  • Offensive or disruptive behavior may result in suspension of membership privileges.

These rules help us maintain a welcoming and respectful tennis community. Thank you for following them.
