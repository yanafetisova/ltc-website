General Information

Club Tournaments
We’re excited to welcome you to our club tournaments — a fun and competitive opportunity for all full-year members to play, connect, and enjoy the tennis season together!

  • Entry fee: $20 per person
  • Additional event: $15 per person
  • Doubles: $20 per player ($40 per team)
  • Includes: Guaranteed two matches and a Saturday BBQ
  • Who can join: Full annual members only
  • Minimum: 4 players or teams required per event

To register:
Please send an email to lethtennisclub@gmail.com with:

  • Full names of all players
  • Email and phone number for each player
  • Event name and category (e.g. Men’s Singles, Mixed Doubles)

If you’d like to play doubles but don’t have a partner, just let us know — we’ll try to match you with someone.

Each draw requires at least 3 entries to run. If a draw doesn’t reach the minimum, you’ll have the option to switch to another event or withdraw.

Important:
Make sure you're available to play throughout the tournament weekend (Friday evening through Sunday). The exact schedule will depend on the number of entries.

Payment:
Please send your fee by e-transfer to lethtennisclub@gmail.com. You can also pay at your first match (cash or cheque).

Deadline to register:
Entries must be received by Tuesday of the tournament week at 11:59 pm. No late entries will be accepted — please register on time!

Prizes will be awarded to winners and runners-up.

We look forward to seeing you on the courts!
