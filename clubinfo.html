About Us

Our Facility

Located a stone’s throw from Henderson Lake and the Nikka Yuko Japanese Gardens, our facility offers a number of amenities to cater to any tennis player.

With 6 courts, ball machine, viewing deck, night lights, club house, washrooms, kitchen, and BBQ, the Lethbridge Tennis Club is the perfect venue for lessons and social functions, whether you’re a competitive, casual or beginner tennis player.

Established in 1887, we are the longest running tennis club in Alberta.
